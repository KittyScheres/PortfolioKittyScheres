Wombat Combat

The wombat combat FPS engine is an engine based on the quake engine and it uses trench broom as its level editor. I worked on this project with a team of 7 programmers for the duration of half a school year.

Table of contents

  • Level loading
  • Physics

Level loading

One of my responsibilities for this project was to load the data stored in the .BSP files and make it usable for our engine. For this, I looked through the source code of the quake engine, and with some help, I managed to implement the loading of levels.

Physics

For the physics in our engine, we make use of the bullet physics library. Implementing this was originally somebody else their task, but when they were unable to take care of this task, I took over the task. While working with Bullet in our project we ran into many issues where Bullet would crash when certain collisions happened. I eventually managed to fix these issues and in the end, we were able to deliver a stable enough build of our project which was greatly enjoyed at playday.
